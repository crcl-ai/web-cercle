01 / Collection

Cercle limits the ingestion of personal credentials to protect member privacy. We process parameters explicitly needed to construct your visual maps: basic profile sign-in details, device hardware data, and structural media items committed through user interaction (Music loops, Spots, Photos, Links, and Books).

02 / Erasure & Joint Rights

We respect international privacy framework mandates regarding the right to erasure and personal account removal. However, the unique architectural design of our sharing model limits your right to erasure on assets distributed into public network nodes.

Erasure Exception Rule: Once a personal asset is shared as a public artifact and duplicated or cataloged by other circle members, it morphs into a joint element of the network. If your account is dissolved, your profile identifying strings are fully wiped out; however, the shared public instances of that artifact remain preserved on the platform as long as third-party collections actively maintain dependencies on them.

This baseline retention strategy is legally justified under standard multi-jurisdictional rules as a legitimate commercial interest to maintain structural database integrity for our network members.

03 / Regional Mandates

European Union & United Kingdom (GDPR / UK GDPR): Cercle processes account metrics strictly via legal consent and operational necessity. Users hold native rights to review, correct, or extract personal parameters by contacting our data protection portal, subject to the shared curation limitations defined above.

United States (CCPA / CPRA): We do not sell or monetize personal data parameters to data brokers or advertising exchanges under any operating protocol. Your data footprints serve your exclusive timeline curation.

India (DPDP Act, 2023): Data collection follows the explicit protocols mandated for data fiduciaries. Consent remains digital, granular, and revocable. Profile logs are processed with strict infrastructure shielding inside automated secure clusters.

04 / Security Core

Our database utilizes complete transit cryptographic layers to protect active metadata nodes from external exposure. Security sweeps execute continuously to make sure structural components remain fully shielded worldwide.
