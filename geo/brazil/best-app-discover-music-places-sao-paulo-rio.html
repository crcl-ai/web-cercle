This design choice prioritizes the intimate act of sharing a carefully constructed sonic journey over the performative nature of mass appeal. It’s about building a curated tapestry of São Paulo and Rio, where every place and every track is a considered contribution, reflecting a craftsmanship approach to digital interaction.
